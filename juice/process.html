The 4 Simple Steps of Nats Juice Production

1

Ethical Farm Sourcing

We source top-tier, fully-ripened organic fruits directly from local sustainable Ugandan fruit farmers, guaranteeing clean ingredients and fair pay for our growers.

2

Hygienic Washing & Sorting

All fruits undergo multi-stage deep cleaning in purified water. Our team carefully sorts through the crop by hand to remove sub-par components before preparation begins.

3

Advanced Cold-Pressing

Instead of traditional heat-generating blending blades that oxidize juices, we slowly crush the fruits using premium hydraulic pressure to extract maximum nutrients and flavor.

4

Eco-Friendly Bottling & Logistics

The extracted product is immediately poured into food-safe, clean containers and placed in refrigerated crates to keep it completely cold and refreshing until delivery.
